# Chiropractic Care for Back Pain: A Drug-Free Path to Relief

Back pain is the leading cause of workplace disability worldwide, affecting roughly 80% of adults at some point in their lives. Whether it's a dull ache that won't go away or a sharp pain that stops you in your tracks, back pain can severely impact your quality of life. At Thrive Chiropractic, we see patients every day who have struggled with back pain for months — sometimes years — before seeking chiropractic care.

The good news? You don't have to live with it.

## Why Is Back Pain So Common?

Modern lifestyles are working against our spines. Hours spent sitting at desks, poor sleeping positions, repetitive movements at work, and a lack of regular exercise all contribute to spinal misalignment and muscle imbalances. Over time, these factors cause:

- **Muscle strain and tension** in the lower and upper back
- **Spinal misalignments** (subluxations) that irritate surrounding nerves
- **Disc degeneration** from prolonged poor posture
- **Joint dysfunction** in the facet joints of the spine

Many people turn to painkillers for relief, but medication only masks the symptoms — it doesn't address the underlying cause. That's where chiropractic care comes in.

## How Chiropractic Care Treats Back Pain

At Thrive Chiropractic, we use the Gonstead method — one of the most precise and well-researched approaches in chiropractic. Rather than applying generalised adjustments, we identify the exact vertebral segment causing your problem and deliver a specific correction.

Here's what our approach looks like:

### 1. Thorough Assessment

Every patient receives a comprehensive evaluation, including posture analysis, spinal palpation, and — where necessary — X-rays. We don't guess. We find the root cause.

### 2. Targeted Adjustments

Using Gonstead-specific techniques, we correct misalignments in the spine that are contributing to your pain. These adjustments restore proper joint movement, relieve nerve pressure, and allow your body to heal naturally.

### 3. Personalised Treatment Plan

Back pain isn't one-size-fits-all, and neither is our care. We create a tailored plan based on the severity of your condition, your lifestyle, and your goals.

## What Symptoms Can Chiropractic Help With?

Chiropractic care can address a wide range of back pain symptoms, including:

- Chronic lower back pain
- Upper back stiffness
- Pain that radiates into the legs
- Reduced range of motion
- Muscle spasms
- Pain that worsens with sitting or standing

## When Should You See a Chiropractor for Back Pain?

Don't wait until the pain becomes unbearable. If you've been experiencing back pain for more than a few days, or if it's recurring, it's worth getting assessed. Early intervention often leads to faster recovery and prevents the problem from worsening.

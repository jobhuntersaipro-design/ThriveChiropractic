# Chiropractic Care During Pregnancy: Safe, Gentle, and Effective

Pregnancy is one of the most physically demanding experiences the human body goes through. As your baby grows, your centre of gravity shifts, hormones loosen your ligaments, and your spine and pelvis adapt to accommodate an increasing load. For many expectant mothers, this means back pain, hip discomfort, and pelvic tension — sometimes significant enough to affect daily life.

Chiropractic care during pregnancy is safe, gentle, and highly effective at managing these changes. At Thrive Chiropractic, we provide specialised prenatal care to help you have a more comfortable pregnancy from start to finish.

## How Pregnancy Affects the Spine and Pelvis

Throughout pregnancy, your body undergoes remarkable changes:

- **Weight gain** shifts your centre of gravity forward, increasing the curve in your lower back (lumbar lordosis)
- **Relaxin hormone** loosens ligaments throughout the body, particularly in the pelvis, to prepare for birth — but this also reduces joint stability
- **Postural changes** — as the belly grows, the upper back rounds to compensate, often causing mid-back and rib pain
- **Pelvic shifts** — the pelvis may become misaligned as it adapts to the growing uterus, leading to sacroiliac joint pain and pubic symphysis dysfunction

These changes are natural and necessary — but they don't have to be painful.

## Benefits of Chiropractic Care During Pregnancy

### 1. Relief from Back and Pelvic Pain

Lower back pain and pelvic girdle pain are the most common musculoskeletal complaints during pregnancy. Chiropractic adjustments restore proper alignment to the spine and pelvis, relieving pressure on joints and nerves. Many patients report significant pain reduction after just a few visits.

### 2. Optimal Pelvic Balance

A balanced pelvis gives your baby the best chance of moving into the optimal position for birth. When the pelvis is misaligned, it can restrict the space available to the baby (intrauterine constraint), potentially contributing to breech or posterior positioning.

### 3. The Webster Technique

At Thrive Chiropractic, we are Webster-certified. The Webster technique is a specific chiropractic analysis and adjustment for pregnant women that focuses on:

- Balancing the pelvis and sacrum
- Reducing tension in the surrounding ligaments (particularly the round ligaments)
- Creating optimal space for the baby to develop and move

Research supports the Webster technique's effectiveness in promoting pelvic balance, and many midwives and obstetricians refer patients for this care.

### 4. More Comfortable Pregnancy

Beyond pain relief, regular chiropractic care during pregnancy can:

- Improve sleep quality
- Reduce nausea (particularly with upper cervical adjustments)
- Maintain better posture as the body changes
- Support overall nervous system function

### 5. Easier Labour and Delivery

A properly aligned pelvis allows for a more efficient labour. Studies suggest that women who receive chiropractic care during pregnancy may experience shorter labour times and reduced need for interventions.

## Is Chiropractic Safe During Pregnancy?

Yes. Chiropractic care is widely recognised as safe throughout all stages of pregnancy. Our techniques are specifically adapted for pregnant patients:

- **Modified positioning** — we use specialised cushions and table adjustments to accommodate the growing belly
- **Gentle, low-force techniques** — the adjustments are comfortable and carefully controlled
- **No X-rays during pregnancy** — we rely on hands-on assessment and clinical history

## When Should You Start?

You can benefit from chiropractic care at any stage of pregnancy, but earlier is better. Many of our patients begin in the first trimester and continue through to delivery. Regular care throughout pregnancy helps your body adapt to each stage of change with less pain and better function.

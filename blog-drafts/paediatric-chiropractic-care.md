# Paediatric Chiropractic Care: Why Children Benefit from Spinal Health

When most people think of chiropractic care, they picture adults with back pain. But children can benefit from chiropractic just as much — often more, because early intervention sets the foundation for a lifetime of healthy spinal development.

At Thrive Chiropractic, we provide gentle, specialised care for infants, toddlers, and children of all ages.

## Why Do Children Need Chiropractic Care?

Children's spines are subjected to more physical stress than many parents realise:

- **Birth itself** can be traumatic — particularly with assisted deliveries (forceps, ventouse, or caesarean), which can place significant force on a newborn's neck and spine
- **Learning to walk** involves countless falls, bumps, and tumbles
- **Playground activities** — climbing, jumping, and rough play create repeated impacts
- **Heavy school bags** carried unevenly or for extended periods
- **Screen time** — children are developing "tech neck" at younger ages than ever before
- **Sports participation** — contact sports, gymnastics, and other physical activities carry injury risk

These micro-traumas accumulate. Without assessment, spinal misalignments can develop silently and affect a child's health and development over time.

## Signs Your Child May Benefit from Chiropractic Care

Children don't always complain about pain the way adults do. Look for these signs:

- **Postural asymmetry** — one shoulder higher than the other, head tilt, or uneven hips
- **Restricted neck movement** — difficulty turning the head to one side (common in infants)
- **Frequent ear infections** — may be linked to upper cervical misalignment affecting drainage
- **Colic and unsettled behaviour** in infants
- **Sleep difficulties** — trouble falling asleep or staying asleep
- **Growing pains** — recurrent leg or knee pain, particularly at night
- **Bedwetting** beyond the expected age
- **Headaches** — increasingly common in school-age children
- **Poor concentration** — which can sometimes be linked to nervous system dysfunction

## How Paediatric Chiropractic Differs from Adult Care

Children are not small adults. Their spines are still developing, their joints are more flexible, and their bodies respond differently to treatment. Paediatric chiropractic care is adapted accordingly:

### Gentle Techniques

The adjustments we use for children are extremely gentle. For infants, the amount of pressure applied is comparable to what you'd use to test the ripeness of a tomato. There's no "cracking" — just precise, light corrections that guide the spine into proper alignment.

### Age-Appropriate Assessment

We assess children differently depending on their age:

- **Newborns and infants** — we check for birth-related spinal stress, particularly in the upper cervical spine, and assess feeding, sleeping, and behavioural patterns
- **Toddlers and young children** — we look at developmental milestones, posture, gait, and symmetry
- **School-age children and adolescents** — we assess posture, spinal alignment, and any sport-related or growth-related concerns (including early signs of scoliosis)

## Common Conditions We Help With

### Colic

Colic — characterised by persistent, unexplained crying in infants — can be distressing for both baby and parents. Research and clinical experience suggest that gentle spinal adjustments, particularly to the upper cervical and thoracic spine, can help reduce colic symptoms.

### Sleep Difficulties

A properly functioning nervous system supports healthy sleep patterns. When spinal misalignments interfere with nervous system function, sleep can be affected. Many parents report improved sleep for their children after chiropractic care.

### Growing Pains

While often dismissed as a normal part of childhood, growing pains can sometimes indicate biomechanical issues in the spine, pelvis, or lower limbs. Chiropractic assessment can identify whether structural factors are contributing.

### Ear Infections

Recurrent ear infections in children may be linked to dysfunction in the upper cervical spine, which can affect the drainage of the Eustachian tubes. While chiropractic doesn't treat the infection itself, improving cervical function can support the body's natural ability to manage and prevent them.

## When to Start

There's no minimum age for a chiropractic check-up. We see newborns within days of birth, particularly after difficult or assisted deliveries. The earlier we can identify and address any spinal issues, the better the outcome.

Many parents bring their children for regular check-ups — not because there's a problem, but to support healthy development and catch any issues early.

## Investing in Your Child's Health

Children's bodies are remarkably resilient, but they're not invincible. The spinal stresses of childhood — from birth through adolescence — can have lasting effects if left unchecked. Paediatric chiropractic care is a safe, gentle, and proactive way to support your child's health and development.
